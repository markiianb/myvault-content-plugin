# MyVault Content & Distribution Strategy
## Q1 2026 Update

*Last updated: January 26, 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines MyVault's updated content and distribution strategy following a comprehensive audit of our brand system, LinkedIn performance, and blog content.

**The Core Shift:** We're moving from a professional-heavy, fear-based content approach to a balanced, confidence-based strategy that serves all three audiences equally.

| Old Approach | New Approach |
|--------------|--------------|
| Default to families, mostly serve professionals | Equal weight: ~33% each audience |
| Fear-based hooks (breaches, threats) | Confidence-based hooks (control, calm) |
| Industry commentary focus | Product demonstration focus |
| Long-form posts only | Mixed formats (carousels, video, polls) |
| ~90% professional content | Balanced across all three |

---

## Our Three Audiences

All three audiences share a common need: **intelligent organization + privacy + control over their digital life.**

They arrive through different doors, but want the same thing.

### Audience 1: Tech-Forward Families

**Who:** Parents (35-55), $100K-$300K+ income, comfortable with tech but overwhelmed by volume.

**Entry point:** Family protection, photo chaos, emergency preparedness

**The moment:**
> "47,000 photos. Files everywhere. What if something happens to me—can my spouse find what they need?"

**Content hooks:**
- Photo organization and memories
- Emergency preparedness (non-morbid)
- Subscription management
- Family sharing

---

### Audience 2: Security-Conscious Professionals

**Who:** Professionals (28-50), tech/finance/legal/healthcare, power users of privacy tools.

**Entry point:** Privacy architecture, data sovereignty, zero-trust

**The moment:**
> "I read the privacy policy. I know what 'free' really costs. And I can't verify how my data is handled."

**Content hooks:**
- Zero-knowledge encryption
- Data ownership and export
- AI transparency
- How it actually works

---

### Audience 3: High Net Worth Individuals

**Who:** Affluent individuals (45-70), $1M-$50M+ net worth, complex financial lives.

**Entry point:** Estate organization, document consolidation, legacy planning

**The moment:**
> "Documents with my attorney, accountant, three advisors, two properties. My kids don't know where anything is."

**Content hooks:**
- Estate consolidation
- Legacy planning
- Family coordination for adult children
- Premium organization

## What to STOP Doing

### 1. Leading with Fear

**Current pattern:**
> "16,200 AI breaches in 2025..."
> "87% of decisions poisoned in 4 hours..."

**Why it's not working:**
- Attracts only security professionals (1/3 of audience)
- Creates anxiety, not trust
- Violates brand voice ("confidence, not fear")
- Low engagement

**Replace with:**
> "Your data never leaves your vault."
> "You decide what MyVault can access."
> "Organization that happens automatically."

---

### 2. Over-Indexing on Industry News

**Current pattern:**
- Sam Altman commentary
- Geoffrey Hinton warnings
- Stanford vs MIT AI reports

**Why it's not working:**
- Positions MyVault as commentator, not solution
- Doesn't demonstrate product value
- Appeals only to professionals

**Replace with:**
- Product demos showing the transformation
- Before/after scenarios
- "How MyVault handles X" content

---

### 3. Writing 400+ Word LinkedIn Posts

**Current pattern:**
- Dense, stat-heavy posts
- Wall of text
- No visual hook

**Why it's not working:**
- LinkedIn algorithm favors shorter content
- Users scroll past long posts

**Replace with:**
- 150-250 words max
- One clear idea per post
- Visual (image, carousel, video) required

---

### 4. Ignoring Family & HNW Content

**Current mix:** ~90% Professional, ~5% Family, ~5% HNW

**New mix:** ~33% each

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn

| Element | Guideline |
|---------|-----------|
| **Length** | 150-250 words |
| **Visual** | Always include (carousel preferred) |
| **Hook** | First line must stop the scroll |
| **CTA** | Question or clear action |
| **Hashtags** | 3-5, always include #MyVault |
| **Posting time** | Tue-Thu, 8-10am or 12-1pm |

### Blog

| Element | Guideline |
|---------|-----------|
| **Length** | 800-1200 words (not 2000+) |
| **Structure** | Hook → Problem → Solution → MyVault → CTA |
| **Subheadings** | Every 200 words |
| **Product mention** | Must show MyVault solving the problem |
| **Audience** | Rotate: Family → Professional → HNW |

### Newsletter

| Element | Guideline |
|---------|-----------|
| **Length** | 500-800 words |
| **Tone** | Thoughtful, personal, one big idea |
| **Structure** | Insight → Implication → What we're doing |
| **Frequency** | Weekly or bi-weekly |
